How to make meat balls with white source

Step 1

Ground beef and ground pork, milk, cream, bread crumbs, onions, white mushroom, garlic, salt and black pepper, butter, olive oil, baguette.

Step 2

Mix ground beef and ground pork, a little bit milk and Bread crumbs in a bowl, smash them at least 20 times, then add salt and black pepper and mix them again.Hot pan with cold olive oil, fired meats with high heat for 3 minuses. It doesnâ€™t need to be cooked well, medium well is ok.

Step 3

Make a new hot pan, add butter and melt the butter, then add garlic, sliced onion fired. Add cream and milk, and white mushroom, boil them for 20 minutes with low fire.

Step 4

Add medium well meat balls into the white saurce, boil them together for another 10 minutes with middle heat.

Step 5

Slice three baguette, but them on the side of the plate, then put five meat balls at the center, sauce and mushroom on the top.
